## Publication boundary

- Candidate text is never copied automatically from the reference application.
- A teacher reviews pedagogy, terminology, answer uniqueness, accessibility, and rights before publication.
- Imported content uses the tracked validator/preview/confirm transaction and creates versions.
- Historical sessions keep their frozen versions; later edits cannot change past answers or scores.
- Production receives only approved ledger rows and never receives legacy Auth users or learning records.
